Philosophy for All

Mission

The primary goal of the Philosophy Honor Society is to provide philosophy to all, to make it easily accessible and to spread knowledge of its existence to those who may do well with it.

To find out more about this goal, and how chapters are supposed to accomplish it, you can read the Phi Lambda Phi constitution : outlining the general goals and procedures of a member chapter
