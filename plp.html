Philosophy for All

Mission

The primary goal of the Philosophy Honor Society is to provide philosophy to all, to make it easily accessible, and to spread knowledge of its existence to those who may do well with it.

To find out more about this goal, and how chapters are supposed to accomplish it, you can read the Phi Lambda Phi constitution : outlining the general goals and procedures of a member chapter

Standard Curriculum

If you have already made a chapter and would like to introduce the subject of philosophy to your members, as is a part of Phi Lambda Phi's goal, a standard curriculum has been created with lecture notes based on various subjects in modern philosophy. Give it a try, and if you would like to explore more in-depth topics, there are plenty of lecture notes from formal courses available online, or you can create your own based on your members' needs.
